Inner Dialogue — Support

Inner Dialogue is a private journal — write or speak what's on your mind, and hear it back in your own words. If you need help, you're in the right place.

Questions, problems, or feedback? Email hello@innerdialogue.app — or use Feedback & support on the Me tab inside the app. We read every note.

Writing and reflecting

On the Today tab, type an entry or tap Speak to talk it out (your voice is transcribed on your device). To hear a reflection, pull down and tap “Reflect on this.” The first time, you'll be asked to allow the AI, in plain words — nothing is sent until you agree.

The AI, and your privacy

You can turn the AI off any time, and you can erase everything it has written about you, without deleting your journal: Me → Account & data → Erase what the AI wrote. Full details are in the Privacy Policy.

Inner Pro subscription

Inner Dialogue is free with two reflections a day. Inner Pro unlocks unlimited reflections (monthly, quarterly, half-yearly, or yearly, with a free trial on the annual plan).

Manage or cancel: open the iOS Settings app → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → Inner Dialogue. Canceling takes effect at the end of the current period. (Deleting your account does not cancel the subscription — cancel it here.)

Restore a purchase (new phone, or reinstalled the app): open the paywall and tap Restore.

Delete your account

To permanently erase your account and everything in it — entries, reflections, portrait, subscription record — go to Me → Account & data → Delete account. This cannot be undone. Note that deleting the app alone does not delete your account.
