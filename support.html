Common questions

The app can't hear me. Aloud needs microphone access. Open iOS Settings → Aloud → Microphone and make sure it's turned on, then restart the app.

How do I manage or cancel Aloud Pro? Subscriptions are handled by Apple. Open iOS Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Aloud to change or cancel your plan. Cancelling keeps Pro active until the end of the current paid period.

How do I restore a purchase? On the Aloud Pro screen, tap Restore Purchases while signed in with the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.

How do I delete my data or account? In the app, go to Me → Privacy & Data. You can clear what Koi has learned about you, or delete your account and all of its server data.
